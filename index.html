Who Are We?

Ghost Kitchens is a groundbreaking and innovative company connecting talented, local chefs with local hungry customers. Opening a restaurant isn’t for everyone for a number of reasons, ... the biggest of which is cost. Between space rental, decor, dishes, equipment, and staff (good luck finding workers during this shortage) to name a few things, the cost of opening a new restaurant can easily start at $275,000 according to sage.com. Our mission at Ghost Kitchens is for you to forget about all of those costs and focus on your passion and what you do best; create.

What do we do?

In a nutshell, Ghost Kitchens handles all of the marketing, advertising, website, guest checkout and other costs associated with running a business, for a small fee. We are simply a bridge that connects you to customers. ...We’re designing our website to be extremely user friendly and straightforward. Each chef will have their own admin page that will feature a bio, a personal photo, and their menu.

What am I responsible for?

The food side of things is quite literally your business. You choose how many days a week you want to cook and what you want to cook. Think of it as your restaurant, ...but in the comfort of your home. You build your menu, provide a description and picture for each dish and set pricing.
